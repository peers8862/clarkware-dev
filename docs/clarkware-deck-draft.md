# Clarkware Deck Draft

## Slide 1: Title

**Clarkware**  
Facility-aware operational software for industrial work

Subtitle:
Integrated Production Environments for evidence capture, job-linked coordination, and auditable human-and-AI operations.

## Slide 2: The Problem

Industrial work is still fragmented across:

- disconnected tools
- informal notes
- generic chat
- manual exports
- reconstructed reporting

Result:

- slower problem-solving
- weaker traceability
- reduced trust
- harder quality review
- operational knowledge loss

## Slide 3: The Insight

The highest-value industrial software opportunity is not another generic dashboard.

It is a system that records and relays the real story of work inside a facility: what happened, who did it, what evidence exists, what was discussed, what AI suggested, and what decision followed.

That is what Clarkware is built to do.

## Slide 4: What Clarkware Is

Clarkware is CLARK's customer-facing software family for industrial operations.

Its first product form is the **Integrated Production Environment**, or **IPE**:

- workstation-level operational environment
- job context
- note capture
- machine-linked evidence
- job-linked messaging
- auditable AI support
- facility-level reporting from the same record

## Slide 5: Why Now

Several enabling conditions now exist at the same time:

- modular workspace technology such as Theia
- standards-based messaging and presence such as XMPP
- growing demand for connected frontline workflows
- increasing willingness to use bounded AI support in industrial operations
- pressure for stronger traceability, accountability, and operator visibility

## Slide 6: The Wedge

Clarkware begins with a narrow but compounding wedge:

- one workstation-level IPE
- one meaningful evidence-import path
- one job-linked messaging model
- one auditable AI recommendation loop
- one manager-facing reporting view

This is intentionally narrower than trying to replace ERP, MES, SCADA, and collaboration software all at once.

## Slide 7: Product Walkthrough

At the workstation level, Clarkware combines:

- active job context
- observation notes
- test and diagnostics artifacts
- issue-linked messaging
- AI summaries and recommendations
- event history

At the facility level, Clarkware derives:

- job visibility
- issue visibility
- handoff visibility
- quality and test exceptions
- AI items pending review

## Slide 8: Why This Is Believable

Clarkware is built on proven substrates and adjacent product patterns:

- Theia for modular work environments
- XMPP for messaging and presence
- industrial platforms for connectivity and reporting
- connected-worker software for frontline workflow support
- industrial AI patterns for bounded context-aware assistance

The innovation is in integration, workflow design, and trust architecture.

## Slide 9: Why CLARK Has The Right To Build It

Clarkware is not a detached SaaS thesis.

It is aligned with CLARK's broader node model:

- real first deployment environment in Niagara Assembly
- direct fit with CLARK's industrial operating model
- support for licensing, governance, and repeatability across nodes
- stronger software leverage tied to real operational environments

## Slide 10: Technical Advantage If It Works

If Clarkware becomes the place where jobs, notes, messages, artifacts, and AI actions are linked, it creates:

- stronger process memory
- better operator and manager visibility
- auditable AI usage
- higher switching costs tied to record value
- repeatable node software standards

## Slide 11: Niagara Pilot

First proof point:

- one diagnostics or test workstation
- one real evidence-import path
- one remote expert workflow
- one AI summarizer or routing loop
- one facility reporting surface

Pilot success means real jobs flow through the system with live evidence, live messaging, and live reporting.

## Slide 12: Roadmap

1. Workstation proof
2. Facility visibility
3. Station expansion
4. Node standardization
5. Cross-node intelligence
6. Deeper automation later

## Slide 13: Why Investors Should Care

Clarkware is interesting because it is not just another software product.

It can become:

- a licensing layer
- a trust layer
- a process-memory layer
- a coordination layer for independently operated CLARK nodes

## Slide 14: Closing

Clarkware does not need to replace the whole factory stack on day one.

It only needs to prove a narrow workstation wedge that is valuable enough to become the memory and coordination layer of the node.
